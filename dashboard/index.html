Emotion is what the AI understood as your mood from the resume provided. Pro tip: A good resume will have joy and a little bit of anger the last being associated with strength. For more details, check Robert Plutchik's theory on emotions.
Values are the beliefs you have and as a rule of thumb people with similar value tendencies are more likely to get along with each other while people with opposite spectrums tend to have conflicts. For more details, check The Theory of Basic Human Values, developed by Shalom H. Schwartz
The Big Five personality traits, is a technique that maps personality to common language descriptors.
    Openness ( inventive/curious vs. consistent/cautious)
    Conscientiousness ( efficient/organized vs. easy-going/careless)
    Extraversion ( outgoing/energetic vs. solitary/reserved)
    Agreeableness ( friendly/compassionate vs. challenging/detached)
    Emotional Rage ( sensitive/nervous vs. secure/confident)
Needs are universal and directly influence consumer behavior. The twelve categories of needs that are reported here are desires that people hope to fulfill when they consider a product or service. For more details, you can check The Marketing Theory of Needs
